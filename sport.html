The 2016/17 has been defined by the football first team recording some of the greatest sporting achievements in history:

  • LaLiga 2016/17,
  • The Club World Cup 2016,
  • The European Super Cup 2016.

On a financial plane, the titles have been reflected in increased revenues, but also in higher expenses; particularly relating to bonuses paid to sporting personnel, resulting in reduced liquidity.
